Recognizing the New

My creative project includes a series of works that discuss the evolving perspectives and experiences of NYUAD students on campus and around the world as they deal with COVID-19.

Wake-Up, Zoom, Sleep, Repeat

Understanding the repetitive nature of days as a university student with online learning and social distancing and the effort to practice a healthy lifestyle within these constraints.

NYUAD Cat Tour

A fresh yet nostalgic tour of the NYUAD campus from the perspective of NYUAD cats aimed towards students who have been away and missing campus.

View from the Other Side

A contrasting look at the ups and downs of life for students on NYUAD campus and at home and the cycle of missing the other side.
